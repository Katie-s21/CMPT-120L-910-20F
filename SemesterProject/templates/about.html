About Us!

I have been fostering cats for 4 years now with my mom. We have lots of experience.

One of my craziest experiences was one of the cats we were fostering gave birth to 8 kittens in our bathroom! We took care of them for 8 weeks and adopted them out to our family and friends.

We even have five of our own cats. Their names are

    • Ethel
    • Lucy
    • Rosie
    • Ainsley
    • Lincoln

Lincoln is a kitten we were fostering over the summer. He has some health issues and we decided to adopt him!

It is interesting to see all of the cats interact as they are different ages and have different personalities.
